Pineapple Pizza

Best Pizza Recipe

This Pizza rocks

This is a basic Pineapple Pizza Recipe but if one does not eat pork you may substuitue any toppings with toppings you favor.

List of Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Mozerella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • garlic
  • Pineapple
  • Green Peppers
  • Black Olives
  • Banana Pepper

Steps

  1. Knead Dough
  2. Add dough to Pizza pan
  3. Add Sauce
  4. Add Garlic
  5. Add Mozerella
  6. Add pineapple
  7. Add Black Olives
  8. Add Green Peppers
  9. Add Banana Pepper
  10. bake at 450 for 35 minutes or until crust is golden brow
